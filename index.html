Frequently Asked Questions
How do I start a new bingo group?
Tap the purple + button on the Home screen. Give your group a name, pick an emoji or photo, then invite your friends. Once everyone has locked in their cards, you can start the game and set an end date.
Can I change my bingo card after locking in?
No — once you tap "Lock in", your card is set for the duration of the game. This keeps things fair for everyone in the group. Make sure you're happy with your squares before locking in!
How does scoring work?
Your score is calculated as: bingos × 100 + total squares marked. Bingos are worth the most, with squares marked acting as a tiebreaker. The leaderboard updates in real time as players mark off squares.
What is the FREE SPACE?
The centre square on every bingo card is a FREE SPACE — it counts as already marked for everyone. This helps complete diagonal bingos and gives every player a head start.
How do I remove ads?
You can remove ads with a one-time in-app purchase available in the app. Go to Profile → Remove Ads to unlock an ad-free experience permanently.
Why can't I see my friend's card?
Cards are only visible once the game has started (all players locked in and admin has tapped Start Game). During the Draft phase, cards are private to keep things fun and competitive.
How do I report inappropriate content?
All uploaded photos are automatically scanned for inappropriate content before being saved. If you encounter anything that seems inappropriate, please email us at ferroveraint@gmail.com and we'll investigate immediately.
Can I play BingoSocial across iOS and Android?
Yes — BingoSocial works across both iOS and Android. Players on different devices can be in the same group with no issues.
