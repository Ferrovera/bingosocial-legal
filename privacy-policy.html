Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Email address — used to create and authenticate your account
  • Display name and username — shown to other users in groups and the activity feed
  • Profile photo — optionally uploaded to personalise your profile
  • Group photos — optionally uploaded when creating or managing a bingo group
  • Bingo card content — the prompts and text you enter on your personal bingo card

1.2 Information Generated by Your Use of the App

  • Game activity — squares you mark, bingo completions, and group activity
  • Friend connections — friend requests sent and received, and your friends list
  • Group membership — groups you create, join, or are invited to
  • Device identifiers — used by our advertising partner (Google AdMob) to serve ads
  • Usage and analytics data — app interactions collected via Firebase Analytics

1.3 Information Collected Automatically

  • Crash reports and diagnostics — collected to identify and fix technical issues
  • IP address and general location — collected by Firebase and Google AdMob as part of normal service operation
2

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Enable the core game functionality — bingo cards, group management, friend connections, and activity feeds
  • Display your profile photo, name, and username to other users in your groups and friends list
  • Moderate uploaded images using Google Cloud Vision SafeSearch to prevent inappropriate content
  • Show you advertisements via Google AdMob (see Section 5)
  • Improve the app through analytics and crash reporting
  • Respond to your support requests
3

How We Store Your Information

Your data is stored using Google Firebase, with our Firestore database and Storage bucket located in the australia-southeast1 (Sydney) region. Firebase is a Google service subject to Google's own privacy and security standards.

The following data is stored in Firestore:

  • User profiles (name, username, email, profile photo URL)
  • Bingo cards (prompts, marked squares, lock status)
  • Groups (name, members, game phase, start/end dates)
  • Activity feed events
  • Friend and join requests

Profile and group photos are stored in Firebase Storage. We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data will be removed from our systems within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it by law.

4

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share your information only in the circumstances described below.

4.1 With Other Users

Your display name, username, and profile photo are visible to other users you interact with — including friends, group members, and users who view the activity feed within your shared groups. Your bingo card content is visible to members of your groups.

4.2 With Third-Party Service Providers

  • Google Firebase (Firestore, Auth, Storage, Analytics) — core infrastructure and authentication
  • Google Cloud Vision API — image moderation (photos are analysed before upload; we do not retain Vision API responses)
  • Google AdMob — advertising (see Section 5)

4.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, or if we believe in good faith that such disclosure is necessary to comply with a legal obligation, protect our rights, or protect the safety of our users.

5

Advertising (Google AdMob)

BingoSocial is free to use and is supported by advertising through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use device identifiers (such as your Advertising ID) and usage data to show you personalised ads.

On iOS, we will request your permission before allowing AdMob to track your activity across other apps and websites, as required by Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework. If you decline, you will still see ads, but they will not be personalised based on your activity in other apps.

You can opt out of personalised advertising at any time through your device settings:

  • iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking — disable tracking for BingoSocial
  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads — enable "Opt out of Ads Personalisation"

For more information on how Google uses data in advertising, visit: policies.google.com/technologies/ads

Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access — you can view your profile information within the app
  • Correction — you can update your display name, username, and profile photo in the Profile screen
  • Deletion — you can request account deletion by emailing ferroveraint@gmail.com. We will remove your personal data within 30 days
  • Objection to advertising — you can limit ad tracking via your device settings as described in Section 5

To exercise any of these rights, or if you have a privacy concern, please contact us at ferroveraint@gmail.com.

Children's Privacy

BingoSocial is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete that information.

Australian Privacy Law

International Users (GDPR)

Our legal basis for processing your personal data is your consent (for advertising) and the performance of a contract (to provide the BingoSocial service).
